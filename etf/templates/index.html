Evaluation Registry

The Evaluation Registry is a place for recording information about evaluations across government. Civil servants can share plans and findings of evaluations and search for evaluations.

Add an evaluation

You are encouraged to share an evaluation as soon as it is being planned, however it can be shared at any point during the process. You can come back and update it as you have more information or findings.

You should only share evaluations of Official status, nothing Secret or above. The first version of the Evaluation Registry is for civil servants only. You will be able to control if your evaluation is visible to others or not.
